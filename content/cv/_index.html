TB/HIV/Hepatitis and Research Programme Advisor

Manson Unit, Médecins Sans Frontières UK - 2015 to present

When I joined MSF, this was the job I dreamt of holding. Over the years, I’ve gained field experience in Belarus, Russia, Tajikistan, Uzbekistan, South Sudan, DR Congo, and Sierra Leone, mixing short strategic visits with longer more comprehensive field support.

The job also requires justification and planning of a wider portfolio of TB across one section of MSF both through a technical medical and humanitarian lens.

The strong implementation focus of the position requires strengths in field support, guideline development, research methodology and execution as well as important advocacy roles within policy and technical committees.
